Make Professional Product Images For Amazon Listings











About:

Hi! I'm Takib, a freelancer in graphic design and Photoshop editing, I have a great experience in this field, also I'm good at photo retouching, any photo retouch: faces, bodies, products...

I had an eBay store where I have been doing all my products images retouching and editing all by myself, I try to make the product images look attractive and appealing as possible to get the buyers to click without thinking, I work on clients products images as if they were mine, so you can rest assured that you'll get the best work I can provide, my only goal here is to please every one of my customers by delivering high-quality work in time.



My Services:

  • Create Infographic design for your product images.
  • Remove and make background white/ transparent/ custom
  • Create a natural shadow/reflection.
  • Remove dust and scratches
    • Add logo/labels/texts.
    • Image sharpening.

    • Color replacement and correction.
    • Enhancing and adjusting color/lightening contrast.
    • Resize images to get the perfect size for your listing/website.
